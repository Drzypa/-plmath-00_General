\section{Mathematics resources, international institutes and major facilities}

 This is a new contributed topic on major facilities and resources 
for mathematics and mathematical/theoretical physics, including computational/supercomputing
and theoretical paper web-archiving:

\begin{itemize}
\item  Princeton Advanced Theoretical Research Institute
\item  IHES, in Paris, France
\item  \PMlinkexternal{The Cavendish Laboratory}{http://www.phy.cam.ac.uk/},Department of Physics,

\item  Supercomputing National Centers, e.g.: NCSA at Urbana, IL
\item  Beckmann Advanced Research Institute, Urbana-Champaign, Illinois
\item  RAL= \PMlinkexternal{Rutherford Appleton Laboratory}{http://www.scitech.ac.uk/About/Find/RAL/Introduction.aspx} 
       near Didcot, Oxfordshire
\item  Max Planck Institute, Germany
\item  Institute for Theoretical Physics Research, Germany
\item  Trieste Advanced Theoretical Physics Research Institute
\item  High-density Energy Research Institute, Kyoto, Japan
\item  CERN, International preprint archives in Geneva 
\item  ANL web-archiving center of mathematical physics/theoretical papers
\item  National Centers for Nanotechnology, Material Science Centers
\item  IHEP in Russia
\item  Institute of Mathematics of the Romanian Academy, in Bucharest, Romania
